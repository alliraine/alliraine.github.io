transition timeline

this is a post taken from an old blog written before system discovery. it's being reposted to preserve this writing and our thoughts and feelings at the time
note: this was last updated 10/21/2022 and may never be updated again
  • 11/24/2021 - I started to question if I was cis
  • 11/29/2021 - I came out to some friends as non-binary or genderfluid. During this time I also was researching what it meant to be trans and started to wonder if I was trans instead.
  • 12/17/2021 - I finally accepted to myself that I was trans. A few days before I told my wife I thought I was but this was the day I felt like I accepted and it fully realized who I am
  • 12/22/2021 - I started with a therapist and contacted Planned Parenthood to enquire about HRT
  • 12/28/2021 - I had my appointment with Planned Parenthood and got prescribed 10mg of EV IM weekly, 100mg of progesterone daily, and 50mg of spiro daily.
  • 12/29/2021 - I officially started HRT.
  • 01/05/2022 - Told my kids that I was transitioning and they started calling me "mom".
  • 01/15/2022 - Came out to all family
  • 01/16/2022 - Officially 100% out publicly
  • 01/21/2022 - Applied for legal name change
  • 01/26/2022 - Appeared before a judge and officially became Allison
  • 07/11/2022 - Legally changed my gender to Female
  • 10/21/2022 - Consultation for bottom surgery (GCS/SRS)
