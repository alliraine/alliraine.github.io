Your Transphobia is Killing People

this is a post taken from an old blog written before system discovery. it's being reposted to preserve this writing and our thoughts and feelings at the time
note: this was written 3 months into transition and originally published March 24th, 2022
Transphobia as defined by Dictionary.com as "an aversion or hostility to, disdain for, or fear of transgender people." Actions of transphobia include but are not limited to refusing to call people by their name, their preferred pronouns, saying they will ALWAYS be their gender assigned at birth, and so much more.

You may feel you are just doing your duties as Christian refusing to do these things or maybe you just want to own the libs. However, your actions are killing people. Your actions are killing my friends. You may not be pulling a trigger or committing a hate crime as defined by law, but your blatant disregard for others is causing a large number of suicides within the trans community.

According to the American Academy of Pediatrics transgender male adolescents reported the highest rate of attempted suicide (50.8%), followed by non-binary adolescents (41.8%), and transgender female adolescents (29.9%). These are kids who are attempting to take their own lives to escape your transphobia.

Suicides in the transgender populous isn't exclusive to just adolescents. According to a 2015 survey, 51% of transgender adults had attempted suicide within a year. I personally know of several friends who are dealing with suicidal ideations, and know of one who has attempted suicide within the past two months. Never have I had to be so concerned about my friends killing themselves.

So next time before you go to share an ill-informed post about how trans women shouldn't be allowed to play sports with other women, or how someone is and always will be a man (even if it's satire), consider how your actions may take a life. That may be the last straw to push someone over the edge ending their precious life.
