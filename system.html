what is this about?

to put it simply. we are a dissociative system. we are a single body and a single brain shared by multiple beings. the person you know is one of many people who live in this body and who help keep the system of alli running and alive. we all have our roles in life and all have different preferences, and viewpoints on the world. if you think we sound crazy, believe us, we have felt and thought the same thing. however the evidence is undeniable that we all exists as separate but semi coherent system.

does this mean you have DID (Dissociative Identity Disorder)?

we have not been formally diagnosed. we do not think we meet the full diagnosis criteria for DID but we do meet the criteria for OSDD-1b. if we had to put a label on it we believe we are an OSDD-1b system.

when/why did this start?

this is rather unclear and we are working on trying to understand ourselves and our past trauma. we were emotionally abused as a child and on atleast one occasion physically abused beyond spanking under the age of 6. we also have a history of being raised in a cult-like level of religion, having parents with untreated mental disorders, including an eating disorder, and some transference of sexual trauma from a young age (under 4). we believe that we split into a traumagenic system prior to age 6 as a coping mechanism.
